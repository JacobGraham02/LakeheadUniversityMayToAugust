CONJUGAISON AU PRESENT/ CONJUGATION IN PRESENT TENSE

in French, verbs are conjugated differently regarding their classification:

1st group:  -ER regular ending (aimer, détester, étudier, ...)

2nd group: -IR regular ending (finir, grandir, ....)

3rd group: Irregular verbs (all verbs that don't belong to the 1st or the 2nd group)
